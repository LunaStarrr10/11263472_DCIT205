Our Courses

Courses We Offer

Embark on a journey into technology's forefront with our Computer Science courses at the University of Ghana. Guided by seasoned faculty, our varied curriculum spans coding, data science, and artificial intelligence. Catering to all levels, our programs provide an engaging learning environment, preparing you for success in the ever-changing landscape of computer science. Join us in shaping the future of technology!

Undergraduate Programmes

Under the guidance of experienced instructors, our undergraduate computer science courses offer comprehensive programs that integrate academic understanding with practical application, equipping students for the quickly changing technology field, including data science and coding.

MPhil and Msc programmes

Our computer science MSc and MPhil programs provide advanced coursework and practical experience to help students enhance their knowledge and lead innovation in the rapidly changing field of technology and healthcare integration.

PhD Programmes

Our PhD programs in computer science give interested students an in-depth look at cutting-edge research topics while encouraging creativity and critical thinking. Work together with academics, make a difference in innovative projects, and influence computer science's future.
